# E.C.H.O. — Player Guide

Welcome to E.C.H.O. Here's what you need to know.

## What This Is

E.C.H.O. is a shared narrative experience. You and other players are dropped into the same world, but each of you perceives it differently. A mystery unfolds through your choices. No one has the full picture — the answer only comes together at the end.

## How It Works

1. **You'll receive a DM** with a few questions about yourself. Answer however you like — a few words is enough. Your answers determine your role.

2. **You get a role.** Each role perceives different things:
   - **Observer** — You notice visual details, spatial patterns, and light.
   - **Listener** — You hear sounds, voices, rhythms, and silences.
   - **Keeper** — You find objects, textures, mechanisms, and traces.
   - **Anchor** — You sense atmosphere, temperature shifts, and timing.

3. **Each chapter, you read a scene** written for your role. You'll encounter details only you can notice.

4. **At the end of each chapter, you face a decision.** This is the core of the game. Your choices shape what happens next and have real consequences.

5. **Respond with your choice.** A sentence, a few words, whatever feels right. There's no specific format required.

6. **The story continues** once all players have responded. Your next chapter reflects the consequences of your decision.

## What to Expect

- The story is immersive and sensory. Details matter.
- You might notice things that seem out of place — details that don't belong to your scene. That's intentional.
- Other players are in the same world, making their own choices. You won't see their chapters, but you'll feel the effects.
- The finale brings everything together. That's when the full picture becomes clear.

## Tips

- **Trust your gut.** There's no walkthrough. Just choose what feels right.
- **Be specific.** "I open the left door" gives the story more to work with than "I continue."
- **Pay attention to details.** The things that feel off are often the most important.
- **Take your time.** There's no timer. The next chapter waits for everyone.

## Language

You choose your preferred language during intake. The story adapts to you. Other players in the same session can play in different languages.
